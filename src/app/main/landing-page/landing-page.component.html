About
Empowering Environmental Guardians

At Green House, we are committed to creating a cleaner and healthier world. Our mission is to provide you with the tools and information you need to monitor and combat environmental pollution. Our app offers real-time pollution monitoring and system alerts to empower you to take action and make a positive impact. With Green House, you can stay informed about air and water quality, make informed decisions, and contribute to a greener, more sustainable future. Join us in our journey to protect and preserve our planet. Together, we can make a difference.

Welcome to Green House, where environmental empowerment is at your fingertips. Our app enables you to take control of your environment by monitoring pollution sources, accessing vital statistics, and engaging in eco-challenges to reduce pollution.
